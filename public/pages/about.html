About Shree Classes

Nurturing Excellence Since Our Founding

Who We Are

Shree Classes is focused on English 8-10 and Marathi 3-10 student excellence.

Shree Classes is a trusted coaching institute located in Kalyan West, near Birla College (adjacent to Mayur Bar). We specialize in providing quality education for students in English 8-10 and Marathi 3-10. Our institute was founded with a single mission: to bridge the gap between classroom learning and comprehensive understanding with consistent mentoring.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality education that builds confidence, discipline and results.

Our Values

Dedication, integrity and genuine care for each student and family at Shree Classes.

Our Approach

Concept-first teaching, regular assessments and personalized doubt-solving for every learner.
